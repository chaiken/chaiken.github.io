Other parts you may need to buy upfront

  • Keyboard and mouse must be connected to the expansion board via the USB OTG mini-B connector, not the mini-A's, one of which is for the serial console. The hubs I see have output, host-side connectors that are mini-A. Therefore, you will likely need either a mini-A/mini-B cable or some adapters. As of this writing, mini-A/mini-B cables are a bit hard to find even in Silicon Valley. Do yourself a favor and order one when you buy your Gumstix!
  • You must use a powered USB hub.
